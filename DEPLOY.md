# Quick Deployment Guide

## Fastest Way: Netlify Drop (No signup required!)

1. **Go to**: https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. **Drag and drop** the `index.html` file
3. **Copy the URL** they give you (e.g., `https://amazing-app-123.netlify.app`)
4. **Open that URL on your phone** and bookmark it!

That's it! Your app is live and accessible on your phone.

---

## Alternative: GitHub Pages (Free, permanent)

### Step 1: Create GitHub Repository
1. Go to https://github.com/new
2. Name it (e.g., "meditation-app")
3. Make it **Public**
4. Click "Create repository"

### Step 2: Upload Your File
1. Click "uploading an existing file"
2. Drag `index.html` into the upload area
3. Click "Commit changes"

### Step 3: Enable GitHub Pages
1. Go to **Settings** tab
2. Scroll to **Pages** section
3. Under "Source", select **"main"** branch and **"/ (root)"** folder
4. Click **Save**
5. Wait 1-2 minutes, then visit: `https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/meditation-app/`

### Step 4: Access on Phone
- Open the URL on your phone's browser
- Bookmark it or "Add to Home Screen" for app-like experience

---

## Add to Phone Home Screen

### iPhone:
1. Open the app in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Name it "Just Breathe"
5. Tap "Add"

### Android:
1. Open the app in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (3 dots)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
4. Name it "Just Breathe"
5. Tap "Add"
